🎣 Phishing Defense

Learn how to identify and avoid fake messages and scams

📌 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where hackers trick you into giving personal information like passwords, bank details, or OTPs using fake emails, messages, or websites.

⚠️ Why is it Dangerous?

  • Steals your passwords and accounts
  • Can access your bank details
  • Leads to identity theft
  • Spreads malware

🚨 Types of Phishing

  • Email Phishing: Fake emails pretending to be companies
  • SMS Phishing (Smishing): Fake messages with links
  • Website Phishing: Fake login pages
  • Spear Phishing: Targeted attacks on specific people

🔍 How to Identify Phishing?

  • Suspicious email address
  • Spelling mistakes
  • Urgent messages like “Act now!”
  • Unknown links
⚠️ Example: "Your account will be blocked! Click here now!"

🛡 How to Stay Safe

  • Never click unknown links
  • Check website URL carefully
  • Do not share OTP/password
  • Enable 2FA

💡 Pro Tip

Always hover over links before clicking to check the real URL.
