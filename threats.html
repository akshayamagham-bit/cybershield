Common Cyber Threats

Stay informed about the most dangerous cyber threats and learn how to recognize them before they cause harm.

📧

Phishing

High
Fraudulent emails or messages designed to steal sensitive information like passwords and credit card numbers.
🦠

Malware

Critical
Malicious software including viruses, trojans, and spyware that can damage or steal data from your device.
🔒

Ransomware

Critical
Malware that encrypts your files and demands payment for decryption.
👤

Identity Theft

High
Stealing personal information to impersonate someone and commit fraud or misuse identity.
📡

Man-in-the-Middle

High
Intercepting communication between two parties to steal or manipulate sensitive data.
💳

Credit Card Fraud

High
Unauthorized use of credit card information to make purchases or withdraw money.
📱

Mobile Threats

Medium
Malicious apps, SMS phishing, and vulnerabilities targeting smartphones and tablets.
🗄

Data Breach

Critical
Unauthorized access to databases containing sensitive personal or corporate information.
