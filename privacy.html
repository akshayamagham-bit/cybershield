🔐 Data Privacy

Learn how to protect your personal information online

📌 What is Data Privacy?

Data privacy refers to protecting your personal information like emails, phone numbers, passwords, and financial details from unauthorized access.

⚠ Why is it Important?

  • Prevents identity theft
  • Protects sensitive information
  • Ensures safe online activity

❌ Common Risks

  • Data leaks and breaches
  • Tracking by unknown apps
  • Sharing personal info on social media

✅ Best Practices

  • Limit sharing personal data
  • Use privacy settings in apps
  • Avoid unknown websites
  • Use secure connections (HTTPS)

💡 Pro Tip

Always check app permissions before installing and avoid giving unnecessary access.
